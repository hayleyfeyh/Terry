About


Terry is a Master Goldwell Color Artist & Editorial Stylist. 


He has been in the industry for over 10 years and has competed in multiple global hair color competitions. Renowned stylist , Charlie Price, has featured Terry in the well known online Beauty Underground Magazine and worked with him personally at Mercedes Benz Fashion week in NYC.

Terry specializes in curly hair as well having been certified by Deva Curl & Ouidad making him knowledgeable in curly hair cutting, curling, and styling. His in-depth education with these specified brands as well as Arrojo, Beth Minardi, Paul Mitchell, Brazilian Blowout, Davines, & Redken makes Terry a force to be reckoned with in the industry. His passion and creativity truly sets him apart!
